"Wardrobe Management is the app that allows the user to manage his wardrobe according to his wish list. It aims to provide facility like checking out the combinations according to various events and selecting amongst them, the best of all. The event list comprises of events such as Casual, Formal, Wedding and Festival. Our app aims to present an avatar that would help user to place clothes onto it according to the pre-chosen event and then test various combinations. The user can also upload his own photo from gallery and then check the combinations on it as well. After generating the desired combination, user can share the generated combination to his friends via social networking sites like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter etc. User can login into the app via their google account. User can also like the generated combination and can view the most liked combination."
